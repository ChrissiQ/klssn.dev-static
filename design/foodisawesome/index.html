A Little Introduction

March 24, 2013

I created this website in order to log and display some of my cooking. All photos were taken by me. I'm not the greatest photographer, and I wish I had more photos of my cooking. As time goes on, I'll try to update this site with more recipes and photos. Some of my favourite recipes, unfortunately, I don't have any photos for yet. I'd like to include them, but I can't cook everything at once!

Once I cook some more dishes, I will add more and try to keep up.

My name is Chrissi Klassen. I'm a student at SAIT and I'm working on this website for my course. I'm not an artist! I didn't draw my own logo- I got it from a free clip art website. The credits for it are in the HTML page comments.
