Who are we?

We are a student at SAIT learning how to invent amazing technologies. We're at the forefront of the industry of making molecules connect to other molecules in really weird ways. Sometimes we feel like we're made of stars or something.

We like cats, hedgehogs, pet fish, video games, computers, food, shelter, breathing, living, existing, and we don't really like dying.

Someday we hope to work somewhere that also really likes all the things we like and doesn't like all the things we don't like.
